The Estee Lauder Companies

Presidential Associate Intern, La Mer E-Commerce

July-August 2019

Overview

During the summer of 2019, I was selected for the Estee Lauder Presidential Associate Internship Program, a competitive 10- week talent based learning program. For my day to day assignment, I was placed on the La Mer North America E-commerce team, where I analyzed the brand’s online sales across their top performing retailer accounts. My main project specifically focused on analyzing La Mer’s Fiscal Year 2019 online sales to highlight key drivers of business and sales activations.

Approach

Online retailer sales are impacted by a number of different factors such as:

  • Online sales promotions
  • On-site merchandising and User Experience
  • Site benefits (for example, free shipping)
  • Site giveaways

I analyzed how each of these factors affected La Mer's sales on 7 different retailer accounts in FY19. Based off my FY19 insights, I created strategic recommendations for La Mer’s FY20 online retailer plan.

Result

I created a Retailer playbook that highlighted sales activations for each account and presented my findings to top executives at the company.
